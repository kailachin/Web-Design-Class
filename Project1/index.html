Pairings

Honey pairs well with most cheeses but it espeically tasted amazing with softer cheeses like Brie, blue cheese, camebert, or goat cheese

Fruits blend well with cheese by adding a sweet, tart flavor to the cheese. Apples go well with sharp cheddar, figs or pears with blue cheese, grapes with brie or gouda, and berries with goat cheese

Jam provides fruit in the consistency of honey and it tasted delicious with a variety of cheeses

Wine pairs well with cheese based on intensity. Softer cheeses with a lighter wine and firmer cheeses with a bolder wine
